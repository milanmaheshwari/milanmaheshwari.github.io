About Me

Hello again! My name is Milan Maheshwari.
I am currently a pre-final year student at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee and a self-taught product designer. I draw pleasure from solving people’s problems, I like exploring the world and I believe that simplicity and creativity go hand in hand.

I joined IIT Roorkee as a Chemical Engineer because of a decent rank in the entrance exam. After a while, I started losing interest in the things taught here and realised it wasn’t my cup of tea. Meanwhile, I was exploring different things like programming, design, gaming, etc. It was in 2017, I got a real taste of design when I joined the campus group SDSLabs, which helped me build my basics. The projects I did here plus the concept and freelance projects helped me develop my problem-solving skills. I have also done a few internships and got a taste of how things work in the real world.

Work Experience

I’ve worked on some really good projects with campus groups, startups and well established companies which have added significantly to my learning curve.
